EXPLORE OUR DIVERSE PROGRAMS

Academic programs designed to develop skilled professionals, critical thinkers, and future leaders.

ENGINEERING

RBPU offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering and technology courses, including B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D degrees in various specialisations such as Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, IT, AI, Data Science, Cyber Security, etc. These programs combine theoretical foundations with practical labs, projects, and industry-aligned curriculum to prepare students for technical careers.

MANAGEMENT & COMMERCE

RBPU offers Management and Commerce programs including BBA, B.Com (Hons.), MBA, and M.Com with specialisations such as Business Analytics, Digital Marketing, and Logistics & Supply Chain Management, focusing on practical skills and industry readiness.

COMPUTER APPLICATION

Computer Applications at RBPU offers BCA and MCA programs with specialisations in areas such as Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, and Cloud Computing, preparing students for diverse IT careers.

PHARMACEUTICAL SCIENCES

Pharmaceutical Sciences at RBPU offers programs like D.Pharm, B.Pharm, Pharm.D, and M.Pharm, approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), ensuring quality education and professional eligibility in the pharmacy sector.

HEALTH & ALLIED SCIENCES

Health & Allied Sciences at RBPU offers industry-oriented programs in healthcare and allied domains, focusing on practical training, clinical exposure, and skill development to prepare students for professional roles in the health sector.

LAW & HUMANITIES

Law programs at RBPU, approved by the Bar Council of India (BCI), include BA LL.B., BBA LL.B., LLM , and LL.B., designed to provide comprehensive legal education and professional readiness for legal practice in India.

ELIGIBILITY

Engineering & Technology Programs

Eligible Stream: Non-Medical (PCM)
Students must have completed 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics. (Applicable to Engineering , Agriculture , Pharmacy & technical specialization related programs)

Medical, Nursing & Health Sciences

Eligible Stream: Medical (PCB)
Students must have completed 12th with Physics, Chemistry & Biology. (Applicable to Nursing, Pharmacy, Allied Health Sciences & Medical programs)

Management, Arts & Other Programs

Eligible Stream: Any Stream
Students from Medical, Non-Medical, Arts or Commerce streams are eligible. (Applicable to Management, Commerce, Fashion, Design, Media, Law, Humanities, Social Sciences, Hospitality, Animation etc.)

SCHOLARSHIPS FOR STUDENTS

RBPU Scholarship Entrance Test (RBPU-SET)

Awarded based on performance in the RBPU Scholarship Entrance Test. This scholarship supports academically deserving students through merit demonstrated in the university entrance examination.

Merit Based Scholarship

Offered to students with outstanding academic performance in qualifying examinations or national-level entrance tests. Available across all streams as per university norms.

Education Social Responsibility (ESR) Scholarship

Provided under the University‚Äôs social responsibility initiative to promote inclusive education and offer financial assistance to students from eligible backgrounds.

Armed Forces Merit & Need Scholarship

Designed for bravery award winners, wards or spouses of defence personnel, and wards of award-winning teachers, recognizing service to the nation.

Defence Aspirant Scholarship

Applicable to students associated with NCC, Scouts & Guides, or NSS. Encourages discipline, leadership, and commitment to national service.

Achievers in Sports Scholarship

Awarded to students who have represented at district, state, national, or international levels in sports, supporting both academics and athletic excellence.

Higher Education Scholarship

Exclusively for existing students of Rayat Bahra Professional University (RBPU). Eligible students receive an additional 5% tuition fee waiver for the entire course duration, over and above other scholarships.
